Lake Arrow Head, California

Visited at July,2,2022

Lake arrow head is a very pretty place, its warm in the summer and snows in the winter. It is the perfect place to have a campout. There are many activity center in lake arrow head which offers exiting adventures like zipline, highropes, swimming, archery, ect... Overall, Lake Arrow Head is a wonderful place you should visit.
